## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Community Participation
**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: High
**Mitigation**:
- Regular engagement through events
- Clear contribution pathways
- Recognition and rewards program
- Active communication on social channels

### Risk 2: Maintainer Burnout
**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: High
**Mitigation**:
- Distribute responsibilities across core team
- Clear governance and decision processes
- Rotate on-call/support duties
- Respect time boundaries

### Risk 3: Security Vulnerabilities
**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: Critical
**Mitigation**:
- Implement automated security scanning
- Regular dependency updates
- Security review process
- Rapid response protocol (SECURITY.md)

### Risk 4: Project Abandonment
**Likelihood**: Low | **Impact**: Medium
**Mitigation**:
- Clear project lifecycle (GOVERNANCE.md)
- "Seeking maintainer" labels
- Archive inactive projects clearly
- Document knowledge thoroughly

## Goal Proposal Template

Want to propose a new goal? Use this template:

```markdown
### Proposed Goal: [Goal Title]
**Proposer**: [Your Name/Team]
**Priority**: [P0/P1/P2/P3]
**Timeline**: [Target completion]

**Problem Statement**:
What problem does this solve? Why now?

**Proposed Solution**:
What specifically will we do?

**Success Metrics**:
How will we measure success?

**Resources Needed**:
What resources (time, people, tools) are required?

**Alignment**:
How does this align with our vision/mission?

**Risks**:
What could go wrong? How do we mitigate?
```

Submit as issue with label `type: goal-proposal`
