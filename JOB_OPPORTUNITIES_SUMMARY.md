## Engagement Terms & Conditions

### Payment Structure
- **Milestone-based payments**: 30% upfront, 40% mid-project, 30% on completion
- **Monthly retainer**: For ongoing roles (community manager, project manager)
- **Hourly with cap**: For variable scope projects with maximum budget

### Contract Requirements
- Independent contractor agreement
- Intellectual property assignment to Fused Gaming
- Non-disclosure agreement (NDA)
- Code ownership transfer upon full payment
- 90-day bug fix warranty for development work

### Work Arrangement
- **Fully remote**: All positions are remote-first
- **Flexible hours**: Asynchronous communication preferred
- **Collaboration tools**: GitHub, Telegram, occasional video calls
- **Time zones**: Open to all time zones, some overlap with EST preferred
- **Availability**: 20-40 hours/week depending on role and engagement

### Deliverables & Milestones
- Clear milestone definitions in SOW
- Weekly progress updates required
- Code/documentation review process
- Acceptance criteria defined upfront
- Change request process for scope modifications

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Budget Constraints
**Risk**: Limited funding for all positions simultaneously
**Mitigation**: Phased approach, prioritize P0 critical roles, seek partnerships and grants

### Talent Availability
**Risk**: Difficulty finding qualified contractors in gaming space
**Mitigation**: Competitive rates, interesting projects, flexible work arrangements, open-source contribution opportunity

### Scope Creep
**Risk**: Projects expanding beyond initial estimates
**Mitigation**: Clear SOW with defined milestones, change request process, fixed-price vs hourly decisions

### Quality Control
**Risk**: Remote contractor work quality varies
**Mitigation**: Code review process, milestone-based payments, test coverage requirements, documentation standards

### Intellectual Property
**Risk**: IP ownership disputes
**Mitigation**: Clear IP assignment in contracts, work-for-hire agreements, code review and integration process
